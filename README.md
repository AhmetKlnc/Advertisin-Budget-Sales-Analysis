# 📊 Advertising Budget & ROI Analysis

## 🎯 Project Objective
This project analyzes how advertising budget allocation across different channels (TV, Radio, Newspaper) impacts sales performance. The goal is to identify the most efficient channels and provide data-driven budget optimization recommendations.

---

## 📁 Dataset
- TV Ad Spend
- Radio Ad Spend
- Newspaper Ad Spend
- Sales

---

## 🧠 Key Business Questions
- Which advertising channel drives the highest sales?
- How efficiently is the marketing budget being used?
- Where should the budget be increased or reduced?

---

## ⚙️ Methodology

### 1. Data Preparation
- Cleaned dataset
- Renamed columns for clarity

### 2. Feature Engineering
- Total_Spend = TV + Radio + Newspaper
- Efficiency = Sales / Total_Spend

### 3. Analysis
- Correlation analysis between channels and sales
- Spend vs Sales visualization
- Channel performance comparison

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- 📈 **Radio advertising shows strong correlation with sales**
- 📺 **TV advertising contributes significantly but requires optimization**
- 📰 **Newspaper advertising has minimal impact on sales**

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Reallocate budget from Newspaper to Radio
- Optimize TV spending for better efficiency
- Focus investment on high-performing channels

---

## ⚠️ Important Note

ROI was not interpreted as true profitability because:
- Sales values are not in direct monetary terms
- Instead, relative efficiency and correlation were used

---

## 🚀 Conclusion

Data-driven budget allocation can significantly improve marketing efficiency and sales performance. This analysis highlights the importance of focusing on high-impact channels.
